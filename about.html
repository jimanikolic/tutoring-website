About Me

Hello!

My name is Jim and I am a currently enrolled D1 student at the University of Kentucky College of Dentistry. I graduated spring of 2024 with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in mathematics.

I have been tutoring at the college and high-school level for about five years and have grown to adore it!

I love tutoring because it's a chance to connect with others and share the joy of learning. Being part of someone's educational journey is incredibly rewarding, as I get to witness students grow into more confident, curious individuals.

It's not just about teaching; it's about building meaningful connections and helping people discover their own unique path to success.

I look forward to meeting you!
